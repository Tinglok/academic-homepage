Sams-Net: A Sliced Attention-based Neural Network for Music Source Separation

This webpage is to show some listening examples for our Sams-Net method and the baseline Open-Unmix[1]. It enables different contributions of feature interactions, achieves easier parallel computing and has a larger receptive field compared with LSTMs and CNNs respectively. Experiments show that the proposed framework outperforms most of the state-of-the-art DNN-based methods with fewer parameters.
