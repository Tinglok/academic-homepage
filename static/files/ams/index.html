Natural sound of action

  • Instructions   

    You need to use the following items to make possible common natural actions to make sounds, and photograph the process.Then use one word or sentence to describe the action.

    The standard for objects to make a sound is that the sound is obvious enough and there is no background noise; the interaction between two objects has to be common in daily life.(eg. you put a ceramic cup on a wooden table✔ you hit the door with a cup ×)

    Some object pairs may not be able to interact naturally and make sounds, you can skip these pairs(eg. if you are given a pair: cup and mirror,you can just skip it)

    We encourage you to make more daily actions on the same object-pair, make different soun ds, extra rewards will be offered based on amount of the actions you can find.(eg. if you are given a pair: cup and table, you can make two possible actions :1.put the cup on the table. 2.move the cup on the table. )
