CVC: Contrastive Learning for Non-parallel Voice Conversion

This webpage is to show some listening examples for our proposed CVC and the baseline CycleGAN-VC[1] and VAE-VC[2]. It enables one-sided voice conversion in the non-parallel copora setting, while improving speech quality and reducing training time. More demos are availible in here.
